# A faster way to do the thing

We're proposing a new internal tool that lets the team **ship work in half the time** by replacing the manual handoff between research and engineering.

- **Today:** research findings sit in a Google Doc, eng reads them next sprint.
- **Tomorrow:** findings flow into a structured queue, eng sees them on the morning of standup.
- **Result:** ~2 weeks shaved off every cross-functional cycle.

This deck walks through how it works, when we'd roll it out, and what the team has asked.
