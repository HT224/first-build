# FAQ

## What if a finding is urgent?

Mark it priority `P0`. It bypasses the queue and pages the eng manager directly.

## Does it replace research docs?

No. The doc still lives in Drive. The tool just adds structured metadata and routing on top.

## How do we measure success?

We're tracking *cycle time from finding-shipped to decision-made*. Target: under 5 business days, down from ~14 today.
