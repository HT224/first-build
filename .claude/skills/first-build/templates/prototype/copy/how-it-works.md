# Three steps

## 1. Research uploads findings

Researchers tag findings with a team, a priority, and an estimated impact when they finish a study.

## 2. The tool routes findings to the right team's queue

No more "did anyone see Lily's deck?" — the queue is the source of truth.

## 3. Eng sees findings in the morning standup view

A 30-second scan tells the eng manager what's new and which findings need a decision today.
